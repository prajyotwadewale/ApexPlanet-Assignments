## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

---

## 📂 Dataset

E-commerce Customer Dataset (Cleaned dataset from Task 1)

---

## 📌 Key KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Total Customers

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. 📈 Monthly Sales Trend

* Visualizes revenue trends across months
* Identifies seasonal patterns and growth

### 2. 🌍 Top Countries by Revenue

* Shows highest revenue-generating countries
* Helps understand geographic performance

### 3. 🛒 Top 10 Products by Revenue

* Highlights best-selling products
* Useful for inventory and marketing strategies

### 4. 👤 Customer Segmentation

* Categorizes customers into High, Medium, Low value
* Helps in targeted marketing

### 5. 🎛️ Interactive Filters (Slicers)

* Month filter
* Country filter
* Enables dynamic analysis

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Revenue shows strong growth in later months (Q4)
* United Kingdom contributes the highest sales
* A small set of products drives most of the revenue
* Majority of customers fall under the "Low" segment
